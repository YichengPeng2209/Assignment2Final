Happiness around the world               

The data used is based on Year 2020
😀

Happiness of a country are represented by ladder score which is an index based on the GDP, Social support,Healthy life expectancy,Freedom to make life choices,Generosity and Perceptions of corruption.The value of ladder score is from 0 to 10, where 0 represents the worst possible life and 10 represents the best possible life.

  Finland

Finland was ranked as the best possible living country in 2020. It is one of the northernmost countries in the world and it had almost the cleanest air in the world. Also,people have the opportunity to see the nature's most spectacular light show (Aurora) from September to March.

  Denmark

Denmark was ranked for the second place in the World Happiness Report in 2020. The country is also famous for its high-quality design. It has the most popular building-block toys company LEGO.

  Switzerland

Switzerland was ranked for the third place in the World Happiness Report in 2020. It has long been an emigration hotspot around the world. Nearly 1/3 of the population has migration background. It has the best social security and the lowest crime rate in the world.
